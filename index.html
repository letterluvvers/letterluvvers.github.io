Bristol’s meet-up group for everyone who loves fonts & lettering

We invite lettering artists, signpainters, typeface designers, letterpress printers, calligraphers, typographers, tattooists, font technologists, font fans and more to come together for drinks and networking every two months.

We start with two short talks, then people stay as long as they like for discussion and socializing. The evenings are free, but please sign up on EventBrite so we can judge numbers and contact you in case of important updates. There’s no profit motive, just bringing people of related passions together.
